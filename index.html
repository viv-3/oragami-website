Introduction to Origami

Origami is the art of paper folding, which is often associated with Japanese culture.
In modern usage, the word "origami" is used as an inclusive term for all folding practices, regardless of their culture of origin.

Oragami Instructions and Diagrams


Step by step diagrams are probably the most popular and easiest way to follow to learn how to fold paper and bring your creations to life. However, finding good instructions to follow through the internet can be difficult. So to make life easier, this website has complied a wonderful list of oragami projects to complete.
